Hallelujah Padedha

Kranthi Aravelli

Released on 15 July, 2026 05:30 PM IST Description: Hallelujah Padedha is one of the songs that define a bit my life, God's grace has never left us in the way that we are in. It carried us since so long and will also carry further until we reach his dwelling place. Testimony: In this song, the very few beginning lines describe the vision that God made me see. That day, during night, I was unable to sleep, so during the day I had memorized the 91 Psalm. So, those were the beginning days just after I gave my life to the lord. I did not know what to do. Looking at the darkness, the cold breeze from the fan on my top, makes me feel frightened. I started recalling what I memorized in 91 psalm. Slowly with complete trust on him, I meditated the Psalm. Suddenly everything in front of my eyes became bright, I saw nothing but only white. I'm seeing this bright light by closing my eyes. Then I saw myself out of my body, I was looking at myself, you know, I was in the lap of this person who wore bright white dress. Everything was dark and he was the light. I saw myself in his lap. That moment I was able to sense myself in the body and out of the body. That was a different kind of experience I cannot share and make you feel. I saw him placing his hands on my cheek while I was sleeping in his lap, the moment he did it, I sensed that touch of love. I still remember that touch. It was filled with peace and calmness. My fears were all gone, Slowly I went into the sleep peacefully. Verses 1: Recently when I was praying to get closer to him more and more. Suddenly Holy Spirit started to remind me of those days where I did not work hard to live with him, I enjoyed living with him. I enjoyed the prayer which now feels as a burden, I enjoyed reading the word, which now feels as opening a heavy book. I was just thinking of Jesus and talking to him all the day, in those days, which I lack that now. Holy Spirit has given me that clear contrast between myself in the beginning and the one whom I am now. This led me into the tears. I did not know what to do, but to just ask his help again, because I know, I cannot do it on my own. I cannot come back on my own. Seeking his help is what as an imperfect human all I can do. That's what is there in the song. Followed by the Verse 2 also. So with my Testimony, I don't know how many stay to read. But I definitely believe that God will touch your hearts and encourage you to get back to your first love through my testimony. It is not of Kranthi or by Kranthi, It is by his Spirit. Thank you for reading this testimony and please pray that I write more spiritually substanced and spirit filled songs rather than just a few piece of lyrics which doesn't contain his spirit.
